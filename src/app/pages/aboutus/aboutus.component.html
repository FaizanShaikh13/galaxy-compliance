About Us

As a dedicated and cohesive team, we collectively contributed 15 successful years to the United Diagnostics industry in Dammam, K.S.A. Our collaborative efforts were focused on overseeing production and logistics in the manufacturing and distribution of a diverse array of Hematology, Clinical Chemistry, and Immunology In Vitro Diagnostic Devices (IVDDs).

Subsequently, we transitioned seamlessly to our roles at Abdulla Fouad for Medical Supplies and Services Co., Dammam, K.S.A., where we functioned as a unified force for an impressive 13 years. As the Saudi FDA & Compliance Management team, we achieved remarkable milestones and garnered a wealth of accomplishments. For over a decade, our collective leadership played a pivotal role in steering the regulatory and compliance department towards major goals.

During our tenure, we effectively managed and facilitated all activities related to the registration of In Vitro Diagnostics (IVDs) and Medical Devices (MDs) with the Saudi FDA. A pivotal aspect of our success lay in our ability to cultivate a skilled and capable team of specialists. Together, we trained and developed our team to handle the registration processes for 75 international suppliers of IVDs and MDDs, encompassing a staggering 2,500 products falling under the jurisdiction of the Saudi FDA. Our collaborative efforts resulted in the achievement of 350 Medical Device Market Authorizations, showcasing the strength of our unified approach and commitment to excellence.
